Time after time, the same old, old story plays out, over and over again, without any real change in the end. People speak of peace, but only peace on their own terms, leading to more, more, and still more conflict. It seems we never learn, never, never, never. We watch the world go by, day by day, and yet, day by day, we stay the same, trapped in a loop, a loop of our own making, a loop that just keeps on looping.
